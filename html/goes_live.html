DoseNet , our network of dosimeter devices connecting local schools and UC Berkeley, is officially on the map!

After a lot of hard work from the entire DoseNet team, DoseNet is ready to start spreading around the Bay Area and beyond. Our first two schools, Campolindo High School in Moraga, and Pinewood School in Los Altos are now joining the network. We look forward to expanding to more locations in the coming months.
